Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need AI to use FileFlow?

No. AI classification is completely optional. FileFlow works fully offline with manual classification and custom rules. AI providers (Claude, OpenAI, Ollama) are available if you want smart suggestions, but they're not required.

What happens to my files?

FileFlow never moves files automatically. Every action requires your explicit confirmation. If you change your mind, you can undo any operation. Files are never permanently deleted — they go to Trash.

Is my data sent to the cloud?

No. FileFlow is local-first. Your files and folder structure never leave your Mac. If you use AI classification, only file metadata (name, extension, size) is sent to your configured AI provider — never the file contents.

What is Johnny Decimal?

Johnny Decimal is a file organization system that gives every folder a unique number (like 21.03). It makes files easy to find and remember. FileFlow supports Johnny Decimal structure out of the box, but you can also use your existing folder structure.

How do I restore a purchase?

Go to Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchase. Your Pro license is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on any Mac.

FileFlow is stuck or not responding

Try quitting and relaunching the app. If the issue persists, check if any files are stored in iCloud and not yet downloaded — FileFlow will wait for iCloud files to download before processing.
